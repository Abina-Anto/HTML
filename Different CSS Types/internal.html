THRISSUR

Thrissur is a city in the south Indian state of Kerala.It'sknown for sacred sites and colorful festivals. In the center isVadakkumnathan Temple,dedicated to Lord Shiva and adorned with murals

The top attractions to visit in Thrissur District are:

  • Athirappilly Waterfalls
  • Guruvayur Temple
  • Peechi Dam
